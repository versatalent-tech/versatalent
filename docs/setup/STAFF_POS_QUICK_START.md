# Staff POS - Quick Start Guide

## For Staff Members

### 1. Login to POS

1. Go to: **`https://yoursite.com/staff/login`**
2. Enter your staff email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be automatically redirected to the POS

### 2. Make a Sale

#### Step 1: Add Products to Cart
- Browse products or use search
- Click on products to add them
- Use +/- buttons to adjust quantities

#### Step 2: Link Customer (Optional)
- Ask customer for their NFC card
- Scan the card
- Customer info appears automatically
- You'll see their VIP tier and points

#### Step 3: Checkout
- Click the "Checkout" button
- Payment dialog opens
- Customer enters card details
- Wait for confirmation

#### Step 4: Complete
- On success: "Payment successful!" message
- Receipt prints (if printer connected)
- Points awarded automatically
- Stock deducted automatically

### 3. Check Stock

- Stock level shown on each product card
- Red badge = Low stock
- Gray badge = Out of stock
- Cannot add out-of-stock items

### 4. Logout

- Click logout button in top right
- You'll be signed out securely

## Common Tasks

### Link a VIP Customer
1. Scan their NFC card at any time
2. Customer info appears in cart sidebar
3. They'll earn points on this purchase

### Remove an Item
- Click the trash icon next to item
- Or reduce quantity to 0

### Clear Cart
- Click "Clear Cart" button
- Confirms before clearing

### Cancel an Order
- Contact admin to cancel paid orders
- Stock will be restored automatically
- Points will be reversed automatically

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Can't login | Check email/password, contact admin |
| NFC won't scan | Check card is active, try again |
| Out of stock | Choose different product or notify manager |
| Payment failed | Ask customer to try different card |
| Slow loading | Refresh page, check internet connection |
